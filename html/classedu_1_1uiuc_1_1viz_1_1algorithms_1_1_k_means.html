Detailed Description

Implements the K-means algorithms as described by Mac Queen in 1967.

<bibtex> J. B. MacQueen (1967): "Some Methods for classification and Analysis of Multivariate Observations, Proceedings of 5-th Berkeley Symposium on Mathematical Statistics and Probability", Berkeley, University of California Press, 1:281-297 </bibtex>

Author
Thomas Abeel
